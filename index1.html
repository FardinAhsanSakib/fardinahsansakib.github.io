Hi there!

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at George Mason University and part of the MasonNLP Research Group, where I am an NSF NRT Fellow. My research focuses on Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, Medical Informatics, and Information Retrieval.

I am particularly interested in applying Large Language Models (LLMs) to health information retrieval and analysis, exploring methods to identify and mitigate bias in LLMs, and enhancing explainability of health-related AI models.

I am in the job market, graduating in May 2026, and looking for research positions as well as internships.

Current Research Focus

My current research focuses on applying Large Language Models (LLMs) to health information retrieval and analysis. I am exploring methods to identify and mitigate bias in LLMs, aiming to improve fairness and accuracy in health-related AI systems.

I am also working on enhancing explainability of health-related AI models to increase transparency and trust in medical applications, facilitating better decision-making by healthcare professionals and addressing the needs of underrepresented populations.
